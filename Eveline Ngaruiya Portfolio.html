About

The person behind the screen

I started as a front-end developer intern in 2021, and by early 2022 I was building WordPress sites full-time at Entwined Media in Nairobi. From there I moved to Hudutech Solution, where I spent two years managing full deployment environments, VPS servers, and client relationships across corporate, e-commerce, and NGO sites — before being asked to step up and run the studio as interim managing director.

Along the way I picked up the rest of what a business actually needs online: social media that doesn't go quiet after the second week, SEO that's more than stuffing in keywords, and design work that keeps a brand looking like itself everywhere it shows up.

What I'm best at is being the person you can hand a messy problem to and trust it comes back sorted — explained in plain English, documented properly, and built so someone else could pick it up if they ever had to.

Right now — building Magento e-commerce stores and running website + digital marketing work directly for clients, alongside ongoing WordPress projects.
